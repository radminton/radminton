About the club

Radminton Badminton is a friendly social badminton club affiliated with Badminton England and welcomes adult players of all abilities.

The club was originally set up to cater for less experienced social players that felt intimidated playing at other clubs. As a result, we welcome beginners who are new to the sport and provide a friendly and relaxed atmosphere which allows new players to improve without pressure!

We play every Thursday throughout the year (except for during the Christmas holidays).
